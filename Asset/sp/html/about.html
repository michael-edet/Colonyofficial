Our Journey

From a shared passion for technology to building digital ecosystems that transform communities

2018

The Beginning

Michael and Ekemini began their journey as independent developers, working on freelance projects while honing their complementary skills in frontend and backend development.

2019

First Collaboration

The brothers teamed up on their first major project, discovering their synergistic workflow where Michael's design-focused frontend complemented Ekemini's robust backend solutions.

2020

Birth of Colony Core

Officially founded Colony Core with a vision to create interconnected digital platforms. Launched their first platform, recognizing the power of specialized digital ecosystems.

2021

Platform Expansion

Successfully launched multiple platforms including Planet Colony and Viral Son, each serving distinct communities while sharing the Colony Core infrastructure and design philosophy.

2022

Community Impact

Their platforms began serving thousands of users, with Freelancers Colony helping professionals find opportunities and Planet Play supporting music creators in sharing their work.

2023

Vision for the Future

Continuing to innovate with plans for mobile applications, enhanced platform features, and expanding their digital ecosystem to serve even more communities worldwide.
