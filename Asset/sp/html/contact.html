Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about working with Colony Core

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on complexity, but typically range from 4-12 weeks for most web platforms and 8-20 weeks for mobile applications. We provide a detailed timeline after our initial consultation and project scoping.

Do you offer ongoing support after project completion?

Yes, we offer various support packages including maintenance, updates, and technical support. Our standard package includes 30 days of post-launch support, with optional extended support plans available.

What is your pricing structure?

We offer project-based pricing tailored to your specific requirements. After understanding your needs, we provide a detailed quote with transparent pricing. We also offer maintenance and support packages for ongoing needs.

Can you work with existing systems or platforms?

Absolutely. We specialize in system integration and can work with your existing platforms to extend functionality, improve performance, or create seamless connections between different systems.

How do you ensure project security and data protection?

Security is built into every layer of our development process. We follow industry best practices, implement encryption, conduct regular security audits, and ensure compliance with data protection regulations relevant to your project.
